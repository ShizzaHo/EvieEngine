===[Arhion 1.0.0]===

Arhion is a separate library integrated directly with EvieCore. 
Arhion provides various tools for working with text data, provides a possibility to add localization to your project and also a preprocessor for TMP allowing you to implement your own modules for TMP image management

Dependencies and requirements for running Arhion: 
- Installed EvieCore at least 1.0.0 with all dependencies installed
