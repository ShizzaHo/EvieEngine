===[ExinAI 1.0.0]===

ExinAI is a standalone library integrated directly into EvieCore. 
ExinAI provides flexible tools for creating AI for your enemies.

Dependencies and requirements for running ExinAI: 
- Installed EvieCore at least 1.0.0 with all dependencies installed
